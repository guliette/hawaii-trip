Kualoa Ranch 古兰尼牧场 🔥

Kualoa Ranch 🔥

侏罗纪公园真实取景地。骑UTV冲进那片你在电影里见过的山谷，恐龙随时会从林子里冲出来。

Where they filmed Jurassic Park. Rip through the valley on a UTV — the same valley you've seen on screen.

⏱ 2h UTV🎫 kualoa.com💲 ~$150
🗺 Maps🎫 Book

Lanikai Pillbox Trail 🥾💎

Lanikai Pillbox Trail 🥾💎

短而美的山脊徒步，俯瞰果冻海般的 Lanikai 和双岛 Mokulua。山顶有两个二战碉堡（pillbox），绝佳拍照点。

Short ridge hike overlooking jelly-blue Lanikai and the Mokulua islands. Two WWII pillboxes at the top — iconic photo ops.

⏱ 1-1.5h RT📏 1.8mi💰 Free
🗺 Maps

Nuʻuanu Pali Lookout 🌬️

Nuʻuanu Pali Lookout 🌬️

600米峭壁俯瞰整条 Windward Coast。从 Kailua 回城途中顺路，风吹过山口的呼啸声像夏威夷在跟你道别。

600m cliff over the Windward Coast. Quick stop on the way back from Kailua — wind roars through the pass like Hawaii saying goodbye.

⏱ 30min🅿️ $3/h
🗺 Maps

Manoa Falls Trail 🌿💧

Manoa Falls Trail 🌿💧

侏罗纪公园般的雨林徒步，穿越巨型竹林和蕨类森林，终点是45米瀑布倾泻而下。檀香山后花园，轻松往返。

Jurassic rainforest trail through giant bamboo and fern groves, ending at a 150ft waterfall. Honolulu's backyard gem.

⏱ 1.5h RT📏 1.7mi🅿️ $5
🗺 Maps

Diamond Head (Lēʻahi) 🥾🌋

Diamond Head (Lēʻahi) 🥾🌋

夏威夷最标志性的火山口徒步。1小时登顶俯瞰 Waikiki 海岸线，蔚蓝太平洋一直铺到天边。需提前预约！

Hawaii's most iconic crater hike. 1hr to the top with Waikiki coastline views. Reservation required!

⏱ 1.5-2h RT📏 1.6mi🎫 Reservation💰 $5
🗺 Maps🎫 Reserve

Tantalus Lookout (Puu Ualakaa) 🌇

Tantalus Lookout (Puu Ualakaa) 🌇

檀香山最佳城市观景台。Diamond Head 到 Waikiki 天际线尽收眼底，日落时分黄金光线染遍全程。本地人的秘密日落点。

Honolulu's best city viewpoint. Diamond Head to Waikiki skyline in one sweep. Golden hour here is pure magic. Local sunset secret.

⏱ 45min💰 Free🌇 Sunset!

🌋 Big Island: Lava + Galaxy Summit

Akaka Falls → Mauna Kea云海 → 火山国家公园 → Kona云雾森林+咖啡 → 追海豚

Akaka Falls → Mauna Kea → Volcanoes NP → Cloud Forest + Coffee → Dolphin Safari

火山国家公园 · Kīlauea 🌋

Hawaiʻi Volcanoes NP 🌋

站在Halemaʻumaʻu火山口边缘，凌晨熔岩红光把整片天空烧成橙色。全美国只有一个地方能看正在喷发的火山——就在脚下。

Stand at the rim as midnight lava glow paints the sky orange. The only place in America with an active eruption — right beneath your feet.

⏱ 2 full days🕐 24h💰 $35/car
🗺 Maps🌋 USGS

Mauna Kea 天文台 🌌

Mauna Kea Observatories 🌌

海拔4,205米，云层在脚下翻滚。太阳消失时银河开灯——土星环、木星卫星，裸眼！免费望远镜。冷到发抖但不想走。

4,205m. Clouds beneath your feet. Milky Way flicks on at sunset. Saturn's rings naked-eye. Free telescopes.

⏱ Full day🌡 -2~8°C⚠️ 4WD only💰 Free
🗺 Mapsℹ️ VIS

Holei Sea Arch 🌊

Holei Sea Arch 🌊

熔岩台地尽头，太平洋巨浪拍碎玄武岩拱门。背后是刚凝固的新大地。地球造陆直播。

Where lava meets ocean. Waves smash a basalt arch. Behind you: brand-new land.

⏱ 30min💰 Included
🗺 Maps

Kīlauea Iki Trail 🥾

Kīlauea Iki Trail 🥾

走在几十米厚的凝固熔岩湖上，像月球表面，中间蒸汽口嘶嘶冒烟。4英里，此生最科幻的徒步。

Walk across a 1959 lava lake. Moon surface + hissing steam vents. 4mi RT.

⏱ 3h📏 4mi💰 Included
🗺 Maps

Pololu Valley Lookout 🏞️

Pololu Valley Lookout 🏞️

绝美山谷+黑沙滩+悬崖海岸全景。Waipio的聪明替代——少20%景色省2小时体力。

Stunning valley + black sand + cliffs — zero hiking needed. Saves 2 hours vs Waipiʻo.

⏱ 45min💰 Free
🗺 Maps

Papakolea 绿沙滩 💚

Papakolea Green Sand 💚

全世界只有4处橄榄石沙滩，美国唯一。沙子是翠绿色的，像碾碎的翡翠。走45分钟进去但嘴会合不上。

One of only 4 green sand beaches on Earth. Sand is crushed olivine gemstone. Jaw-dropping arrival.

⏱ 1.5h RT🥾 2.5mi💰 Free
🗺 Maps

Haleakalā 日落云海 🌅

Haleakalā Sunset 🌅

云海在脚下流动，太阳沉入地平线时橙紫深蓝三色同时染满3千米高空。日落后银河上线。⚠️ 日落无需预约！

Clouds beneath your feet, sky exploding orange-purple-navy at 3,000m. Milky Way after. ⚠️ No reservation for sunset!

⏱ Afternoon🌡 4-12°C💰 $30/car✅ No reservation
🗺 Mapsℹ️ NPS

Waihee Ridge Trail 🥾

Waihee Ridge Trail 🥾

雨林山脊徒步，360度俯瞰茂宜北岸和Kahului湾。云雾从树丛里冒出来环绕着你。4英里往返。

Rainforest ridge with 360° views over North Maui. Clouds drift through the canopy around you. 4mi RT.

⏱ 3h📏 4mi💰 Free
🗺 Maps

Paia 冲浪小镇 🐢🏄

Paia Surf Town 🐢🏄

茂宜北岸的波西米亚冲浪小镇。沙滩上躺着晒太阳的巨大海龟，冲浪文化与热带慵懒完美融合。离岛前的完美收尾。

Maui's bohemian surf town. Giant sea turtles basking on the beach, surf culture meets tropical chill. Perfect send-off.

⏱ 1-2h💰 Free🐢 Turtles!
