Kualoa Ranch 古兰尼牧场 🔥

Kualoa Ranch 🔥

侏罗纪公园真实取景地。骑UTV冲进那片你在电影里见过的山谷，恐龙随时会从林子里冲出来。灰尘糊满脸但笑到停不下来。

Where they actually filmed Jurassic Park. Rip through the valley on a UTV — the same valley you've seen on screen a hundred times. Dust all over your face, grin all over your face.

Lanikai Beach 🌅

Lanikai Beach 🌅

夏威夷公认最美沙滩。白沙+双岛+玛瑙绿水——本地人的私家海滩，这里没有人山人海，只有我们四个和太平洋的日出。

Widely considered Hawaii's most beautiful beach. Powder-white sand, twin offshore islands, jade-green water — a local secret, not a tourist circus. Sunrise here rewires your brain.

Nuʻuanu Pali Lookout 🌬️

Nuʻuanu Pali Lookout 🌬️

站在600米峭壁上俯瞰整条Windward Coast。别穿裙子——这里的风能把头发吹成图腾柱。落地的第一个震撼体验。

Stand on a 600m cliff and stare down the entire Windward Coast. The wind here will rearrange your hair into abstract sculpture. Hawaii's opening statement, and it hits hard.

⏱ 30min🅿️ $3/h

Day 2 · 午后浮潜 Day 2 · Snorkeling

Shark's Cove 🐠

Shark's Cove 🐠

9月底水温28°，能见度全岛最顶。珊瑚礁鱼群密集到你伸手就能摸到——比Hanauma Bay清澈太多，还不用抢票。

Late September water at 28°C, visibility at its peak. Coral gardens so dense the fish basically bump into you. Way clearer than Hanauma Bay and zero ticket-hunting stress.

夏威夷火山国家公园 · Kīlauea 🌋

Hawaiʻi Volcanoes National Park 🌋

站在Halemaʻumaʻu火山口边缘，凌晨的熔岩红光把整片天空烧成橙色。全美国只有一个地方能让你亲眼看到正在喷发的火山——就在我们脚下。两个整天：白天钻500年熔岩隧道，夜晚守着火山口等辉光。

Stand at the rim of Halemaʻumaʻu crater as midnight lava glow paints the sky orange. This is the only place in America where you can watch an active eruption in real time. Two full days: lava tubes by day, fire-glow by night.

⏱ 2 full days🕐 24h💰 $35/car
🗺 Google Maps 🌋 USGS Live Status

Mauna Kea 天文台 🌌

Mauna Kea Observatories 🌌

海拔4,205米，云层在你脚下翻滚。太阳消失时整个银河系开灯——土星环、木星卫星、猎户座腰带，裸眼！免费天文望远镜由退休NASA义工操作。冷到发抖但不想离开。

4,205m above sea level. Clouds roll beneath your feet. The moment the sun vanishes, the Milky Way flicks on — Saturn's rings, Jupiter's moons, Orion's belt, all naked-eye. Free telescopes run by retired astronomers. You'll be freezing but you won't want to leave.

⏱ Full day🌡 -2~8°C⚠️ 4WD only💰 Free
🗺 Google Maps ℹ️ VIS Info

Day 4 · Chain of Craters Day 4 · Chain of Craters

Holei Sea Arch 🌊

Holei Sea Arch 🌊

熔岩台地尽头，太平洋巨浪一遍遍拍碎在你眼前的玄武岩拱门上。背后是刚凝固的新大地——去年还可能是岩浆，今年就成了脚下的石头。地球造陆直播。

Where the lava field meets the ocean. Waves smash against a basalt arch while you stand on land that might have been molten rock just last year. Earth-building, live.

⏱ 30min💰 Included

Kīlauea Iki Trail 🥾

Kīlauea Iki Trail 🥾

走在几十米厚的凝固熔岩湖上，表面像月球表面，中间还有蒸汽口嘶嘶冒烟。4英里往返，是你此生走过最科幻的路。

Walk across a solidified lava lake from 1959. Surface looks like the Moon, steam vents hiss beneath your feet. The most sci-fi hike of your life — 4 miles round trip.

⏱ 3h📏 4mi💰 Included

Pololu Valley Lookout 🏞️

Day 7 · 南端秘境 Day 7 · Southern Edge

Papakolea 绿沙滩 💚

Papakolea Green Sand Beach 💚

全世界只有4处橄榄石沙滩，美国唯一。沙子是翠绿色的，像被碾碎的翡翠撒了一地。走45分钟进去，但走到的那一刻你嘴会合不上。

One of only 4 green sand beaches on Earth, the only one in the US. Sand is literally crushed olivine gemstone. A 45-minute walk in, but your jaw will hit the floor when you arrive.

⏱ 1.5h RT🥾 2.5mi walk💰 Free

Haleakalā 日落云海 🌅

Haleakalā Sunset 🌅

云海在你脚下流动，太阳沉入地平线时橙、紫、深蓝三色同时染满3千米高空。日落后银河上线。⚠️ 日落无需预约，只有日出才要抢名额！

Clouds flowing beneath your feet, sky exploding orange-purple-navy all at once at 3,000m. Milky Way kicks in after sunset. ⚠️ No reservation needed for sunset — only sunrise requires a permit!

⏱ Afternoon🌡 4-12°C💰 $30/car✅ No reservation

Waihee Ridge Trail 🥾

Waihee Ridge Trail 🥾

雨林山脊徒步，360度俯瞰茂宜北岸和Kahului湾。云雾从树丛里冒出来环绕着你——4英里往返，是你在茂宜最值得出汗的事。

Rainforest ridge trail with 360° views over North Maui and Kahului Bay. Clouds drift through the canopy around you. 4 miles round trip — the best sweat you'll earn on Maui.

Ho'okipa Beach Park 🏄

Hoʻokipa Beach Park 🏄

全球风帆/风筝冲浪圣地。站在悬崖上看那些空中翻腾的剪影——不用参与，纯看表演也超值。离岛前的最强收尾。

The world capital of windsurfing and kiteboarding. Stand on the cliff and watch silhouettes flipping through the air. Pure spectacle, zero effort. Perfect send-off before the airport.
